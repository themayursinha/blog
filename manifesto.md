  <section class="manifesto-grid" aria-label="Five core principles">
    <article class="manifesto-card manifesto-card-primary" data-signal="TRUTH">
      <div class="manifesto-card-meta"><span>01</span><span>ROOT AUTHORITY</span></div>
      <h2>Reality Before Narrative</h2>
      <p class="manifesto-card-lead">A beautiful theory that cannot survive evidence is decoration.</p>
      <p>Every model carries assumptions. Name them before they harden into architecture, strategy, or belief. Preserve primary evidence, expose uncertainty, and change your mind when reality disagrees.</p>
      <ul class="manifesto-card-tags" aria-label="Related concepts">
        <li>EVIDENCE</li><li>FALSIFIABILITY</li><li>EPISTEMIC INTEGRITY</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="manifesto-card" data-signal="SYSTEMS">
      <div class="manifesto-card-meta"><span>02</span><span>CAUSAL TERRAIN</span></div>
      <h2>Think In Systems</h2>
      <p class="manifesto-card-lead">Symptoms are outputs. Structure is cause.</p>
      <p>Look beneath events for incentives, feedback loops, bottlenecks, capital flows, and hidden dependencies. Durable interventions change the mechanism producing the failure, not merely its latest manifestation.</p>
      <ul class="manifesto-card-tags" aria-label="Related concepts">
        <li>INCENTIVES</li><li>FEEDBACK</li><li>LEVERAGE</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="manifesto-card" data-signal="CONTROL">
      <div class="manifesto-card-meta"><span>03</span><span>POWER CONSTRAINT</span></div>
      <h2>Bound Power By Design</h2>
      <p class="manifesto-card-lead">Capability without constraint becomes somebody else's risk.</p>
      <p>No model, operator, service, or institution should hold more authority than its task requires. Make power narrow, observable, revocable, and interruptible. Design failure paths before success creates dependency.</p>
      <ul class="manifesto-card-tags" aria-label="Related concepts">
        <li>LEAST AGENCY</li><li>CONTAINMENT</li><li>FAIL CLOSED</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="manifesto-card manifesto-card-wide" data-signal="SOVEREIGNTY">
      <div class="manifesto-card-meta"><span>04</span><span>DURABLE CAPABILITY</span></div>
      <h2>Turn Knowledge Into Sovereign Capability</h2>
      <p class="manifesto-card-lead">Understanding becomes strategic only when it can act.</p>
      <p>Build the prototype. Instrument it. Attack it. Own enough technical depth to inspect critical dependencies, replace components, recover under pressure, and preserve meaningful choice. Invest in systems, teams, and knowledge that compound instead of load that merely repeats.</p>
      <ul class="manifesto-card-tags" aria-label="Related concepts">
        <li>BUILD</li><li>OWN THE BOTTLENECK</li><li>COMPOUND</li>
      </ul>
    </article>

    <article class="manifesto-card manifesto-card-human" data-signal="AGENCY">
      <div class="manifesto-card-meta"><span>05</span><span>FINAL INVARIANT</span></div>
      <h2>Technology Must Enlarge Human Agency</h2>
      <p class="manifesto-card-lead">The universe does not owe us meaning or fairness. Responsibility is ours.</p>
      <p>Technical power is not automatically progress. Judge systems by the understanding they create, the suffering they reduce, the dignity they preserve, and the choices they leave open. Automate execution, but retain the human competence to question, refuse, and redirect it.</p>
      <ul class="manifesto-card-tags" aria-label="Related concepts">
        <li>JUDGMENT</li><li>DIGNITY</li><li>FREEDOM</li>
      </ul>
    </article>
  </section>
